# FuriganaVision Support Page

## App Overview

This app is a camera application that recognizes kanji characters in books and documents and automatically displays furigana (reading guides).

### Features
* Real-time kanji recognition using camera
* High-precision furigana generation using Japanese morphological analysis
* Simultaneous recognition and display of multiple kanji characters
* Multi-language support (Japanese/English)
* Simple and intuitive user interface
* Pause and resume functionality
* Coordinate-based sorting of recognition results (displays text in document reading order)

### Main Use Cases
* Reading assistance for books and magazines
* Learning material comprehension support
* Reading assistance for Japanese language learners
* Confirmation of difficult kanji readings

## How to Use

### Basic Operations
1. When you launch the app, it will request camera permission. Please select "Allow".
2. Please wait a few seconds for the camera to start up (first launch may take a bit longer).
3. Point the camera at books or documents containing kanji characters.
4. When kanji are recognized, furigana will automatically appear at the bottom of the screen.
5. Use the "Pause" button to temporarily stop recognition, and "Resume" button to restart.

### Recognition Tips
* Maintain an appropriate distance so characters are clearly visible
* Use in well-lit environments
* Hold the camera steady to avoid blur
* Avoid complex backgrounds and ensure characters are clearly visible

### Troubleshooting

#### When Camera Won't Start
1. Open Settings app
2. Select "FuriganaVision"
3. Turn on "Camera"
4. Restart the app

#### When Kanji Are Not Recognized
* Check if text size is appropriate (very small or very large text may be difficult to recognize)
* Ensure text is captured clearly
* Improve lighting conditions
* Check if camera lens is clean

#### When App Is Slow or Heavy
* Close other apps to free up memory
* Restart your device
* Update the app to the latest version

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I use this offline?
A: Yes, no internet connection is required. All processing is done on your device.

### Q: Can it recognize handwritten characters?
A: The app is optimized for printed text recognition. Handwritten character recognition accuracy may be lower than printed text.

### Q: Does it support vertical text documents?
A: Yes, both vertical and horizontal text layouts are supported.

### Q: Can it recognize old kanji forms or variant characters?
A: The app is optimized for common kanji recognition. Old character forms or special variants may not be recognized.

### Q: How can I improve recognition accuracy?
A: Please pay attention to the following points:
- Ensure adequate lighting
- Hold the camera steady
- Maintain appropriate distance so characters fit within the screen
- Capture in conditions with simple backgrounds

# VibeMachine Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

VibeMachine ("the App") is a photo editing app designed with privacy as its top priority. This policy explains what information the App handles and why.

## Your photos never leave your device

All image processing in the App — scene detection, skin smoothing, eye/face reshaping, AI face enhancement, and filters — runs **entirely on your device**.

- Your photos and images are never uploaded to any external server
- Face detection results and facial data are never transmitted off the device
- Edited photos are saved to your camera roll only when you choose to save them

## Information we collect

The App uses the following information to improve the service and display ads. None of it includes personally identifying information or the contents of your photos.

### 1. Usage analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase)

To improve the App, we collect usage events (e.g., photo loaded, filter applied, photo saved) with their attributes (scene type, filter name, adjustment strength values) and technical information such as device model and OS version. The advertising identifier (IDFA) is not used.

### 2. Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

To fix bugs, we collect technical information generated when the App crashes (stack traces, device model, OS version, etc.).

### 3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App is provided free of charge and supported by advertising. When ads are displayed, Google AdMob may receive technical information such as your IP address. The App **does not use personalized ads** and does not request tracking permission via App Tracking Transparency (because it does not track you).

If you purchase Remove Ads (a one-time purchase), ads will no longer be displayed.

## Third-party services

The services above are provided by the following company. Please also refer to its privacy policy.

- Google LLC (Firebase / AdMob): https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13.
